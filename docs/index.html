About Ericsson

Ericsson is a Swedish multinational networking, telecommunications and infrastructure company headquartered in Stockholm. Founded in 1876, the company employs around 100,000 people and operates in around 180 countries. Ericsson holds over 42,000 granted patents as of December 2016, including many in wireless communications. Ericsson had 35% market share in the 2G/3G/4G mobile network infrastructure market in 2012.

Our Impact

We provided solutions architecture services for 5 large UI projects that integrated into Ericsson’s complex and multi-faceted OSS/BSS platform.

Our Involvement

Our engineers helped lead the technical vision for 5 large UI projects from a solutions architecture vantage point, using the OSS/BSS paradigm that Ericsson helped define. When Ericsson decided to expand its software offering to include a modern browser-based frontend, it turned to Zero Projects to provide engineering expertise in the venture.

These projects were intended to be value-add purchases for Ericsson’s existing backend APIs, and as such had to integrate with Ericsson’s existing release structure and on-site deployment architecture.

Technical Details

The five internal tools were built using Angular and integrated with existing Java APIs. The build system chosen was Gulp, and the projects were packaged and deployed as WAR files on Apache Tomcat servers. D3.js was used to provide custom directed-graph visualizations.

WIRL - Client Profile

About WIRL

WIRL is a Software-as-a-Service platform that empowers employees and their organizations through continuous feedback. Research shows that 98% of employees find annual performance reviews unnecessary and that employees are dissatisfied with the frequency and quality of the feedback they receive, resulting in high levels of disengagement and turnover. Disengagement costs organizations an average of $2,200 per employee in lost productivity and morale. WIRL solves these problems by making employee feedback simple and safe to request, provide and analyze in real time.

Our Impact

We created a functional single-page SaaS frontend for WIRL, extending existing backend code and creating custom SQL reports for the new project.

Our Involvement

After learning about our expertise in SaaS and frontend development, WIRL decided to engage Zero Projects to help them in the technical portion of a new endeavour: creating a new and improved version of their existing HR SaaS product.

Zero Projects’ software engineers created a functional software product from the design vision laid out by WIRL’s internal UX/UI and product team. The new version of WIRL’s SaaS product integrated with WIRL’s existing code, as well as extending it to better enable WIRL’s clients to use the new and improved features on WIRL’s platform.

Technical Details

Zero Projects built WIRL’s new frontend in Angular 1, which was the most popular frontend framework for single-page applications at the time. It integrated with an existing legacy Ruby on Rails system via JSON/REST-based API calls.

The Ruby on Rails backend was modified to provide new functionality, including extended reporting capabilities and custom SQL queries. Deployment using Capistrano was implemented on DigitalOcean.
